What is Wild Frame?
Wild Frame is a software developed by Birds on the Go, designed for cameras to identify birds in real-time without needing internet access.
Who makes Wild Frame?
Wild Frame is developed by the company Birds on the Go.
When will Wild Frame be launched?
Wild Frame is expected to launch in March 2025.

Wild Frame uses advanced AI technology to detect and identify bird species in real-time without requiring an internet connection.
