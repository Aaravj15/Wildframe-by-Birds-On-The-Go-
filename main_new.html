Our Ideology

Today, a vast majority of wildlife photographers face a really difficult and tricky problem. They cannot identify the bird they are clicking pictures of. Anand Bedi, an enthusiastic wildlife photographer, wanted to solve this problem. He informed his friends and together they came up with a software called Wildframe that can help the photographer to know which bird he is clicking pictures of. This software is installed by the companies that manufacture cameras used by wildlife photographers. When Wildframe is installed in a camera, it can work anywhere in the world without a network. It provides all the information on the specific bird.

Product Features

In-built Bird Identification

Immediately identifies the bird in your frame.

Offline Operation

In a forest, not a problem because this software can operate without any internet.

In-depth Information Provision

It not only identifies the bird but also gives detailed information on the species of the specific bird.
